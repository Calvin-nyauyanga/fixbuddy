Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check the status of my ticket?

You can view the status of all your tickets by logging into your user dashboard and navigating to the "My Tickets" section.

How long does it take for a ticket to be resolved?

Resolution time varies based on the issue's complexity and priority. Our team works hard to resolve high-priority tickets as quickly as possible.

Can I attach files to my ticket?

Yes, you can attach relevant files such as screenshots or documents when you create a new ticket to help our team understand the issue better.
